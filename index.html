Optometry

Institute

The Institute of Optometry at Ayodhya Eye Hospital offers degree and diploma Program in the field of optometric that is affiliated with the U.P State Medical Faculty & Atal Bihari Vajapee Medical University Uttar Pradesh. The program is designed to equip students with the practical knowledge and skills necessary to provide services in ophthalmic techniques and assisting ophthalmologists in OPD, OT, and ward activities.

Some of the key features of the Institute of Optometry at Ayodhya Eye Hospital include:

Experienced and qualified faculty

Strong focus on practical training and clinical experience

Placement assistance for all graduates

Affordable quality education without any discrimination

Well-equipped laboratories with state-of-the-art facilities

The institute is equipped with a wide range of ultra-modern equipment todiagnose and treat all types of eye diseases. It also has a special focus on common problems like allergic infections and simple to complex refractive errors.

Bachelor of Optometry

The Bachelor of Optometry is a four-year professional degree that requires three years of academic instruction and one year of internship for students to graduate. Vision investigation, diagnosis, treatment, and management of diseases and disorders of the visual system are the focus of the medical profession of optometry.

Eligibility:

10+2 or equivalent with or equivalent with min 60%

Candidate having a diploma in optometry/ ophthalmic technique with 60% marks after completion of 10+2 with the science subject they would be eligible to join 2nd-year Minimum Age is 17 years.
